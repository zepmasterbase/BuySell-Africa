What is BuySell Africa?

A private equity marketplace connecting Africa’s business owners, investors, and entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

BuySell Africa exists to unlock Africa’s hidden business value by creating a trusted marketplace where businesses can be bought, sold, and funded transparently. We aim to reduce barriers to capital and accelerate economic growth across the continent.

What We Do

We connect verified business owners with qualified buyers and investors. From startups to established companies, we provide a platform for acquisition, investment, and expansion opportunities across multiple industries.

For Sellers

List your business, get verified, and connect with serious buyers and investors across Africa and globally.

For Buyers

Discover profitable businesses, evaluate financials, and acquire ready-to-scale opportunities.

For Investors

Access curated private equity opportunities across high-growth African markets.
